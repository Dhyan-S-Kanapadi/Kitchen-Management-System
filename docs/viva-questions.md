# Viva Questions

## What is the process in this project?

A food order is treated as a process.

## What is the CPU?

A chef is treated as the CPU because the chef executes the cooking work.

## How do you show CPU scheduling?

The Scheduler page runs FCFS, SJF, Priority Scheduling, and Round Robin, then shows execution order, Gantt chart, metrics, and selection reasons.

## How do you prove semaphore?

The Resources page shows `wait` and `signal` operations on limited equipment and displays semaphore value changes.

## How do you prove mutex?

The Synchronization page shows inventory updates where one order acquires the mutex, updates the critical section, and releases the mutex before another order enters.

## How do you detect deadlock?

The Deadlock page builds a resource allocation graph and detects a circular wait between Order A, Mixer, Order B, and Oven.
